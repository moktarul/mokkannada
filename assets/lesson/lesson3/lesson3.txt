Lesson 3
Conversation 1

A: banni, vaasu, kuutukoli; cennagiddiraa?
Please come Vasu, be seated. How do you do?

B: cennagiddiini saar
Fine sir.

B: namaskaara; niivu cennagiddiraa?
Good morning; how do you do sir?

A: cennagiddini
Fine, thank you.

Conversation 2

A: raaju, idu yaava klaasu?
Raju, which class is this?

B: idu kannada klaasu
This is a Kannada class.

A: idu yaara kannada klaasu?
Whose Kannada class is this?

B: idu namma kannada klaasu
This is our Kannada class.

A: vaasu, aa klaasu yaavdu?
Vaasu, which is that class?

C: adu tamil klaasu
That is a Tamil class.

C: aa klaasu avardu
That class is theirs.

C: ii klaasu namdu
This class is ours.

Conversation 3

A: nimma pustaka yaavdu?
Which is your book?

B: ii chikka pustaka nandu
This small book is mine.

A: yaava pustaka avardu?
Which book is his?

B: adu avardu
That one is his.

A: raaju, ii pennu yaardu?
Raju, whose pen is this?

B: aa pennu nimdu, saar
That pen is yours, sir.

A: alla, aa pennu nandalla
No, that pen is not mine.

A: idu avara pennu
This is his pen.

A: ii hosa pennu nandu
This new pen is mine.

Conversation 4

A: ravi, aa haLe pennu nimmadaa?
Ravi, is that pen yours?

B: haudu, saar
Yes, sir.

B: aa haLe pennu nandu
That old pen is mine.

B: aa baal pennu nandu
That ball pen is also mine.

Additional Conversation

A: ii hosa pennu nimdalvaa?
Isn’t this new pen yours?

B: aduu nandu
That is also mine.

B: aa ella pennugaLu nandu
All those pens are mine.

A: avu avara pustakagaLalvaa?
Aren’t those books theirs?

B: aa eraDu pustakagaLu avardu
Those two are his books.

B: ii chikka nootbuku nimdaa?
Is this small notebook yours?

A: alla, idu nandalla
No, this is not mine.

A: nanna nootbuku doDDu
My notebook is big.

Vocabulary

aa – that

ii – this

siire – sari

yaara – whose

eraDu – two

nandu – mine

avaLdu – hers

yaava – which

yaardu – whose one
